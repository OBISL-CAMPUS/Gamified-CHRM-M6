Theoretical Deep-Dive (Kirkpatrick Model) ▼

Designed by Donald Kirkpatrick, this 4-level architecture provides a systematic ladder to verify human capital progression:

1. Reaction: NPS, pulse surveys & subjective sentiment.

2. Learning: Quantifiable knowledge tests & simulations.

3. Behavior: Real-world job transfer & 360-degree feedback.

4. Results: Macro operational ROI, churn drop, production rate.

HR research demonstrates that most organizations struggle because they only track Level 1 (Reaction) and Level 2 (Learning), failing to measure actual, long-term workplace behavior change or bottom-line business outcomes.
